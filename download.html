Download ScalaCheck

ScalaCheck build artifacts are no longer published on this site. You can find builds published in The Central Repository.

The easiest way to retrieve ScalaCheck is through sbt:

libraryDependencies += "org.scalacheck" %% "scalacheck" % "1.19.0" % "test"

There are still older releases available on this site.
